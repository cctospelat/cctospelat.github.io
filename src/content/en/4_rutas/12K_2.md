A circular route starting from the new Rambleta, passing through Plaza San Jaime, Avenida Fernando el Católico and Calle Concejal J. A. Moreno Calleja, continuing along the Palmar ravine towards Massarrojos as far as Massarrojos metro station; from there, return along the Palmar ravine towards Moncada, linking with the Carraixet ravine, crossing the Bonrepòs-Tabernes Blanques bridge and the pedestrian footbridge over the Vinalesa ravine, back to the Rambleta.
