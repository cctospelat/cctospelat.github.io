At Tos Pelat, we understand sport as a tool for positive transformation, capable of improving people's quality of life and strengthening the social fabric of our environment. Our commitment goes beyond running: we work to promote healthy habits, foster inclusion, and generate a real impact in the community.

We believe in running as a meeting point that brings together people of different ages and levels, creating bonds and reinforcing values such as respect, solidarity, and teamwork.

From the club, we take on the responsibility of actively contributing to the sporting development of Moncada, enriching its offering with initiatives, training sessions, and collaborations that boost citizen participation.

Tos Pelat is, and will always be, at the service of the people of Moncada, committed to accessible, close, and community-focused sport.
