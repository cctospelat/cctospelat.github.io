Tos Pelat continues to honor its colors: red, white, and black. In 1996, its founders decided these would represent the club's identity.

These colors have also become a recognizable symbol of Moncada within the running world, carrying the municipality's name beyond its borders in every race and event.

Today, every runner who wears these colors continues the legacy of those who started this journey, proudly representing Tos Pelat and Moncada wherever they participate.
