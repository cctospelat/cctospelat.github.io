Tos Pelat is a running club born with the aim of promoting sports practice and fostering a healthy lifestyle among its members. 
We believe in running as a tool not only to improve physical fitness but also to strengthen values such as constancy, effort, and personal improvement.

Our club promotes active participation in sports activities, both in Moncada and in other municipalities, collaborating with events, races, and projects that contribute to the development of sports at local and regional levels.

At Tos Pelat, the true engine of the club is its people. We firmly believe in human capital, creating a close, inclusive, and motivating environment where every member, regardless of their level and goals, has a place and an important role within the group.
