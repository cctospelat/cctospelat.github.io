---
nombre: "Speed Intervals"
imagen: "/website/fotos/content/3_entrenamientos/ejercicio_2.png"
ejecucion:
  - "Warm up for at least 15 minutes with easy jogging and mobility drills"
  - "Run 4-6 repetitions of 400 m at 85-90% of your maximum pace"
  - "Rest between intervals for twice the time it took to run each one"
  - "Maintain running form: high cadence, efficient stride"
  - "Cool down: 10 minutes of easy jogging and stretching"
evitar:
  - "Going out too fast on the first intervals"
  - "Shortening recovery time between repetitions"
  - "Running intervals with pain or excessive muscle fatigue"
  - "Skipping the warm-up"
consejos:
  - "Do no more than 2 speed sessions per week"
  - "The last interval should feel as strong as the first"
  - "Stay well hydrated before, during, and after"
  - "Schedule these sessions when you are well rested"
---
