  - "Warm up for 5-10 minutes walking or jogging very lightly"
  - "Keep a conversational pace: you should be able to speak comfortably"
  - "Breathe naturally through both nose and mouth"
  - "Aim for a cadence of 160-170 steps per minute"
  - "Cool down with 5 minutes of very light jogging and stretching"
evitar:
  - "Exceeding 70% of your maximum heart rate"
  - "Starting too fast at the beginning of the session"
  - "Comparing your pace with other runners"
  - "Skipping the warm-up or cool-down"
consejos:
  - "Easy runs should make up 80% of your weekly training volume"
  - "If you can't hold a conversation, slow down"
  - "This is the foundation of all aerobic endurance"
  - "Use these sessions to recover from intense workouts"
